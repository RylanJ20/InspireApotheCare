Staff Pharmacist

Full-Time

Multiple Locations — Elizabethtown, KY area

Join our team of pharmacists providing personalized care to the Elizabethtown community. As a Staff Pharmacist at Inspire ApotheCare Pharmacy, you'll work closely with patients, build lasting relationships, and practice pharmacy the way it should be — with time for every patient.

Requirements

  • Doctor of Pharmacy (PharmD) or equivalent
  • Strong communication and patient counseling skills
  • Commitment to community-centered care
