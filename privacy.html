Information We Collect

The Inspire ApotheCare website is an informational site. We do not collect personal information through this website unless you voluntarily provide it (for example, by emailing us a job application). We do not use cookies for tracking, and we do not sell or share visitor data with third parties.

HIPAA Notice

This website does not collect, store, or transmit protected health information (PHI). For prescription inquiries, medication questions, or any health-related communications, please contact us directly by phone at (270) 763-0303 or visit one of our 5 pharmacy locations.

Inspire ApotheCare complies with all applicable HIPAA regulations regarding the privacy and security of patient health information at our physical pharmacy locations.
