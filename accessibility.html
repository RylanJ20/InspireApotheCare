Accessibility Statement

Our Commitment

Inspire ApotheCare Pharmacy is committed to making our website accessible to all users, including those with disabilities. We strive to meet or exceed the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards.

What We Do

We have taken the following steps to ensure our website is accessible:

  • Semantic HTML structure with proper heading hierarchy
  • Sufficient color contrast ratios for all text
  • Keyboard navigation support throughout the site
  • Descriptive alt text for all meaningful images
  • ARIA labels and roles where appropriate
  • Minimum 44×44px touch targets for mobile users
  • Support for reduced motion preferences
  • Skip-to-content links on every page
  • Readable base font size (18px) designed for our 50+ patient demographic

Need Help?

If you experience any difficulty accessing any part of this website, we want to hear from you. Please contact us and we will work to provide the information you need:

  • Phone: (270) 763-0303
  • Email: Inspiremeds@gmail.com
  • Visit: Any of our 5 pharmacy locations

We are always working to improve the accessibility of our website and welcome your feedback.
